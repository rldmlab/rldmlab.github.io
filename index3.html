Seongmin A Park

I am a project scientist in the team of Erie Boorman at the Center for Mind and Brain, University of California, Davis. I received my Ph.D. in Culture Technology with Jeaeseung Jeong at KAIST and was a postdoctoral researcher in the team of Jean‑Claude Dreher at CNRS.

My research investigates how the human brain implements reinforcement learning and decision making, combining behavioral experiments, neuroimaging (fMRI & EEG), and computational models. I study how complex experiences are transformed into abstract, low‑dimensional representations that support flexible behavior in changing environments.

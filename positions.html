A Postdoctoral Researcher

Representation, Learning, and Decision-Making (RLDM) Lab
Department of Psychology, Virginia Tech

The RLDM Lab is seeking a highly motivated Postdoctoral Researcher to join our interdisciplinary team investigating the neural and computational mechanisms of structured learning and flexible cognition.

About the Lab

Our research combines behavioral experiments, neuroimaging (fMRI, OPM-MEG), and computational modeling to understand how the human brain builds internal models of task structure, transfers knowledge across contexts, and adapts to changing environments.

Project Focus
  • Curriculum learning and knowledge transfer
  • Neural representation of abstract structure and task hierarchies
  • Cognitive map formation and structural generalization
  • Replay and latent structure inference using fMRI and MEG
Responsibilities
  • Design and run behavioral and neuroimaging experiments
  • Develop and analyze computational models
  • Analyze neuroimaging data
  • Prepare publications and present findings
  • Mentor students in the lab
Required Qualifications
  • Ph.D. in a relevant field (e.g., Cognitive Neuroscience, Psychology, CS, Biomedical Engineering)
  • Programming experience (Python, MATLAB, etc.)
  • Experience with neuroimaging, modeling, or machine learning
  • Strong publication record and communication skills
Preferred Qualifications
  • Experience in structured cognition or transfer learning
  • Familiarity with fMRIPrep, MNE, AFNI, or SPM
Location & Application
